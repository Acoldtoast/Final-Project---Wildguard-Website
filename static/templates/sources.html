Organizations Supporting Philippine Endangered Land Species

Philippine Eagle Foundation (PEF)

Supports: Philippine Eagle. The Philippine Eagle Foundation, based in Davao City, focuses on protecting and breeding the critically endangered Philippine Eagle. They collaborate with indigenous communities to safeguard nesting sites, care for injured eagles, and lead education programs on forest conservation. philippineeaglefoundation.org — Donate/Volunteer

Philippine Tarsier Foundation, Inc. (PTFI)

Supports: Philippine Tarsier. Manages the Tarsier Sanctuary in Corella, Bohol; rescues tarsiers, protects habitat, and conducts scientific monitoring. tarsierfoundation.com

Katala Foundation Inc. (KFI)

Supports: Philippine Mouse Deer, Philippine Pangolin, Visayan Warty Pig. Primary organization protecting Palawan wildlife; partners with PCSD for anti-poaching, community education, rescue and research. katalafoundation.org

Negros Forest Park / Talarak Foundation Inc.

Supports: Philippine Spotted Deer & Visayan Warty Pig. Operates conservation breeding centers and releases animals back into protected forests. talarak.org

Philippine Biodiversity Conservation Foundation, Inc. (PBCFI)

Supports: Negros Bleeding-Heart Dove, Philippine Spotted Deer, Visayan Warty Pig. Works on habitat restoration, surveys, and captive breeding. philbio.org.ph

Tamaraw Conservation Program (TCP) — DENR

Supports: Tamaraw. Government-led program that protects tamaraws in Mounts Iglit–Baco Natural Park via ranger patrols, anti-poaching, and community engagement. denr.gov.ph

World Wildlife Fund Philippines (WWF-PH)

Supports: Philippine Eagle, Tamaraw, forest conservation. Works on habitat protection, anti-poaching, and community livelihoods. wwf.org.ph

Palawan Council for Sustainable Development (PCSD)

Supports: Philippine Mouse Deer enforcement and wildlife rescue. Enforces environmental policy across Palawan and works with KFI. pcsd.gov.ph

Haribon Foundation

Supports: Philippine Eagle, Philippine Cockatoo, forest wildlife. Runs forest restoration and awareness programs. haribon.org.ph

Fauna & Flora International – Philippines Program (FFI)

Supports: Philippine Pangolin, Palawan Forest Turtle, and other forest species. Works on anti-poaching, training responders, and protected areas. fauna-flora.org

Daluhay Daloy ng Buhay, Inc.

Supports: Philippine Eagle, forest mammals, Sierra Madre biodiversity. Community empowerment and forest protection programs. daluhay.org

Save Philippine Seas (SPS)

Supports: wildlife crime prevention and habitat protection; education and law enforcement partnerships that help protect endangered species from trafficking. savephilippineseas.org

Mindoro Biodiversity Conservation Foundation, Inc. (MBCFI)

Supports: Tamaraw, Mindoro Bleeding-Heart Dove, cloud rats. Species surveys, forest restoration, and community education in Mindoro. mbcfi.org.ph

Philippine Mammal Project (UP Diliman – IB Program)

Supports: cloud rats, Philippine deer, and endemic mammals; conducts national expeditions and research to inform conservation policy. philippinemammalproject.com

Nagkakaisang Tribu ng Palawan (NATRIPAL)

Supports: Philippine Pangolin, Mouse Deer, and forest wildlife through indigenous forest patrols and sustainable practices. natripal.org

Philippine Native Animal Conservation Society (PNACS)

Supports: endemic mammals, endangered birds, and rehabilitated wildlife; works with zoos and schools on sanctuary and education programs. facebook.com/PNPCSI

Forest Foundation Philippines

Supports: forest ecosystems and wildlife habitat restoration through grants and community projects across the Philippines. forestfoundation.ph
