Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

1. Information We Collect
WildGuard collects information about endangered species, conservation efforts, and user interactions to provide educational content and conservation resources.

2. How We Use Your Data
Your information is used to improve site functionality, send updates about wildlife conservation, and support our mission. We do not sell user data to third parties.

3. Data Protection
We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. All data is treated with confidentiality and used only as described in this policy.

4. Cookies & Tracking
WildGuard uses cookies to enhance user experience. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

5. Third-Party Links
Our site contains links to external conservation organizations. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Review their policies independently.

6. Children's Privacy
WildGuard does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we discover such collection, we will delete the information immediately.

7. Policy Changes
We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted here with an updated date. Continued use of the site indicates acceptance of the revised policy.

8. Contact Us
For privacy inquiries, email info@wildguard.ph or use our Contact form.
