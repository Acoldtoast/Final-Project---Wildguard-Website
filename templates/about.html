Our Mission

WildGuard is dedicated to raising awareness about endangered species and their conservation needs. We believe that through education and community action, we can make a real difference in protecting the world's most vulnerable wildlife populations. Our platform connects people with information about endangered animals, conservation efforts, and organizations working to save these species from extinction.

What We Do

  • Educate: Provide detailed information about endangered species, their habitats, threats, and conservation status
  • Connect: Link donors and supporters with reputable conservation organizations
  • Inspire: Share fun facts and success stories to motivate action
  • Empower: Offer practical tips on how individuals can contribute to wildlife conservation

Our Focus

WildGuard currently focuses on endangered species from the Philippines, a biodiversity hotspot with many endemic species found nowhere else on Earth. These unique animals face threats from habitat loss, climate change, poaching, and human development. By highlighting these species, we hope to raise global awareness and support for their protection.

How You Can Help

There are many ways to support endangered species conservation:

  • Learn about endangered animals through our platform
  • Donate to verified conservation organizations
  • Support sustainable practices that protect natural habitats
  • Share information with friends and family
  • Advocate for wildlife protection policies
